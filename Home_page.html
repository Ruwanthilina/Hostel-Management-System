Wayamba University of Sri Lanka

HOSTEL FACILITIES

The web-based hostel management system is an efficient software solution designed to
streamline and automate hostel operations. It offers a centralized platform for managing tasks such as room allocation, reservations, check-ins and check-outs.
With its user-friendly interface, administrators can easily generate reports. The system eliminates manual paperwork, enhances efficiency, and provides a seamless experience for both hostel staff and residents.
